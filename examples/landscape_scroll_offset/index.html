iOS Scrolls on Orientation Change

iOS scrolls the window after loading in landscape orientation and after rotating from portrait to landscape orientation.

meta viewport window.scrollY after load window.scrollY after rotation
none 19px 20px
initial-scale=1.0, user-scalable=no 20px 21px
minimum-scale=1, maximum-scale=1, width=device-width, user-scalable=no 0px 21px
initial-scale=1.0, user-scalable=no, width=device-width, height=device-height 21px 64px
initial-scale=1.0, minimum-scale=1.0, maximum-scale=1.0, user-scalable=no, width=device-width, height=device-height 64px 64px
